About Text Encryption

Our text encryption tool uses industry-standard algorithms to protect your sensitive information:

  • AES-256: The gold standard in symmetric encryption, used by governments worldwide.
  • RSA: Asymmetric encryption ideal for secure message exchange.
  • Caesar Cipher: A simple substitution cipher for basic obfuscation.
  • XOR Cipher: A lightweight cipher that's fast but less secure.

Note: All encryption/decryption happens in your browser. Your data never leaves your device.
